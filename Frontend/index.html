Safety Protocols & Procedures

Gas Leak Containment

In the event of toxic gas levels exceeding 50ppm, immediate evacuation of Sector 4 is required. Seal primary blast doors and engage local exhaust ventilation (LEV). Do not re-enter without Level A hazmat suit.

Core Temperature Crisis

If reactor core temperature spikes beyond 95°C, automated coolant release will initiate. Manual override for secondary liquid nitrogen flush should be prepared if temperature does not drop within 60 seconds.

Spill Response (Acidic/Basic)

Neutralize spills using emergency neutralizing agents located at stations A through F. Isolate the spill path from active drainage systems. Notify the Environmental Health and Safety off-site team immediately.
